About me

I am currently a researcher at CEA List sin 2017, carrying out research on Natural Languange Processing. I completed my PhD on Automatic Speech Recognition in the Laboratoire d'Informatique Signal et Image (LISIC) of Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale in 2016. My research interests include conversational agents and dialog systems, automatic speech recognition and translation for low-resourced languages, semantic analysis and machine learning.
